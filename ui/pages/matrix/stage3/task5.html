V.3 Land Development Approvals and Permits


Approvals and Permits – Land Development (V.3.)

The land developer needs the following approvals:

  • Environmental approvals (environmental remediation is completed with “no further action” letter).
  • Earthwork permits and inspections.
  • Building permits and inspection.
  • Infrastructure approvals by the city (road network and traffic controls must be approved and inspected).
  • Utility approvals and permits (connecting to the existing electric grid/provider, telecommunication systems, water systems, dry and wet utilities).
