VI.4 Building Development Physical Improvements


Improvements – Building Development (VI.4.)

Design Drawings- Proposed improvements designed by building and landscape architects.

Construction Drawings – Improvements drafted, reviewed, and approved by engineers.

Design-Bid-Build Model:

  • Contractors bid on the contract based on the proposed scope of work, and then
  • Perform the construction necessary to complete the improvements. 

Design-Build Model:

  • Used for ‘fast track” projects.
  • Best if architect, engineers, and contractors have history of working together.
  • Developer must exert cost controls throughout.

Landscape Improvements may be bid separately with a different architect and contractor.

Different architects and contractors are used for:

  • Core & shell
  • Commercial interiors, or
  • Other tenant improvements.
