VIII.6 Building Renovation Sales and Disposition


Disposition – Building Renovations (VIII.6.)

A successful building renovation usually means that the property will be sold to a building operator.

•Same disposition considerations as the building developer selling to the building operator.

When the property can no longer be renovated to accommodate the existing use, it may be sold to a property redeveloper. In which case the following considerations must be met:

•Seller/buyer financing

•Seller representations and warranties 

•Seller's limited liability exposure for the future 

•Contingencies to close
