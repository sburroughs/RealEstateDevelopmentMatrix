Improvements – Building Operations (VI.5.)

Building operators must oversee and maintain the constructed improvements:

  • Service agreements
  • Maintenance agreements
  • Warranty work
  • Monitor operations of systems with benchmark metrics and timely reporting
  • Replace worn out or obsolete equipment as needed

Oversee small-scale building improvements including:

  • Tenant improvements:
    •  New tenants move-in
    • Scheduled tenant replacement work
  • Common area maintenance and renovation:
    • Lobbies
    • Restrooms
    •  Common corridors
    • Parking facilities
  • Building amenities:
    • Fitness centers
    • Conference rooms
    • Roof top terraces
    • Mail rooms and shipping/receiving areas
  • Exterior repairs and upkeep:
    • Window washing and replacement
    • Exterior caulking
    • Façade cleaning
    • Roof repairs
