Environmental – Building Operations (IV.5.)

Continued maintenance and monitoring of building systems is critical.

  • Energy monitoring systems are a great way to check on building efficiency and find energy cost savings.

Keep accurate records of maintenance and use.

  • This will help as reference guides when repairs or renovations are needed.
  • It is also critical to have these records on hand when submitting for certifications.

Require tenant improvements to meet green initiatives.

Encourage tenants and building users to participate and support green initiatives (recycling and decreased energy use requires tenant participation).

  • Sustainability initiatives must be embraced by the building users.
  • Tenant education on how to make sure the systems in place are used and used correctly.
  • The participation of the “People” impacts the “Planet” by lowering waste, water, and energy usage, and impacts the “Profit” by keeping operating costs low.

Research new sustainable technologies to improve life cycle of the building:

  • energy monitoring systems,
  • LED lights,
  • low flow plumbing fixtures.

Continued education and training of new systems and best practices.

  • Environmental and sustainability is newer concept of real estate development, with a lot of change and improvement in the industry each day.
    • Staying current with trends and new technologies, and
    • then incorporating applicable systems into building operations will help to maximize efficiency in building systems.

Certifications: LEED, Energy Star, BOMA360, Green Globes
