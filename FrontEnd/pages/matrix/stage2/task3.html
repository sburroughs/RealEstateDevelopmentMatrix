III.2 Land Packaging Market Studies and Marketing Strategie


Market Studies & Marketing Strategy – Land Packaging (III.2.)

Determine if the expected market conditions be used to justify a change in the property’s legal status.

  • Annexation,
  • Zoning,
  • Subdivision plan

 
Determine if the expected market changes will be embraced by the neighbors, politicians, and local government official.

  • Embrace the market changes as improving the local economy and quality of life.
  • Ignore the market changes as irrelevant to their lives.
  • Fight to stop the market changes to avoid negative effects on the local economy and their quality of life.

Estimate off-site infrastructure changes or modifications will be necessary to accommodate the expected market changes.

  • Can the exiting infrastructure handle the expected market changes?
  • Will additional capacity or new improvements required?
  • Will the exiting infrastructure become obsolete or unnecessary?

Estimate how quickly the changing market conditions will occur.

  • Immediately
  • Near term
  • Long term
