Improvements – Land Packaging (VI.2.)

Land packagers design proposed improvements for many audiences:

  • Planning commission
  • Neighborhood organizations and adjacent land owners
  • Traffic planners
  • Conservation groups

Land packager sometimes have “special” improvements engineered for skeptical stakeholders.

  • What will the bridge look like?
  • What will the path look like?
  • How big will the transit station be?
  • Will it block by view of the ………

Land packagers have a more detailed concept plan that can be included in the entitlement documents along with design guidelines.

  • Road cross-sections
  • Elevation studies
  • Recreational areas
    • Play fields
    • Dog parks
    • Picnic pavilion
