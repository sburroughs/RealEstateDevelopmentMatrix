Efficient ingress and egress to the building. (These are the Big Three.)

  • Cars, bikes, pedestrians,
  • Mail, shipping, receiving
  • Trash collection and removal

Emergency ingress and egress:

  • Emergency vehicles like ambulances and fire-fighting equipment (turning radii)
  • Fire and police access to building systems
    • Water outlets
    • Elevator controls
    • HVAC controls
    • Alarm systems

Efficient accessibility within the building(s):

  • Elevators, escalations, "people movers"
  • Hallways, stairs, suite entrances
  • Emergency routes (stairways, corridors, shelter in place facilities)
  • Americans with Disabilities Act Compliance
    • This is an all-encompassing act that is constantly being updated.
    • It is included in most local building codes.
    • Compliance is not compromised!
