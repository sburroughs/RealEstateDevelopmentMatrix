VI.1 Land Banking Physical Improvements


Improvements – Land Banking (VI.1.)

Land Bankers create the “concept plan.”

Land bankers often plan and design improvements to help land packagers appreciate what can be done with the property, such as:

  • Roads and lots dealing with physical features.
  • Road systems that tie into existing transportation routes.
  • Amenities and common areas for future development.
  • Location of on-site water treatment facilities and/or storm water management facilities.
  • Environmental features which must be embraced or avoided..

Improvements for Interim land uses:

First do not harm!

  • Maintain exiting road and drainage ditches.
  • Maintain gates, fences and security systems.
