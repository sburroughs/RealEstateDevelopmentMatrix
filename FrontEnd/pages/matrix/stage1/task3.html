III.1 Land Banking Market Analysis and Strategies


Improvements – Property Redevelopment (VI.7.)

This is very similar to the Land Banking Stage; not much physical action, but lots of conceptual hard work.

Plan and design new use for the site (with new and/or modified improvements). 

  • “Adaptive re-use” is the new buzzword.
  • A concept plan is helpful.

Need to sell the vision to many stakeholders, public and private.

  • Embrace the past history of the sight (one way or the other).
  • Animated Videos are an invaluable aid; next best is a PowerPoint.
  • Seek out stakeholders; this is the time to make new friends.

Create plan to perform and complete required physical improvements as needed:

  • Property security improvements
  • Public safety improvements

Interim Land Uses: First do no harm!

  • Improvements to accommodate interim land uses should not compromise the future re-development of the property.
  • The neighborhood may not want to give up an amenity that you provided in good faith.
    • Vegetable gardens
    • Play fields
    • Gathering areas
