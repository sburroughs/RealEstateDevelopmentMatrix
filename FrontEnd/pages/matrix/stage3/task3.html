Determine when the building developers will be ready to purchase the finished building site based on the following:

When will the building users be ready to buy or lease the improved property?

  • Since the land developers are preparing lots to be sold to the building developers, the land developers must look beyond to the eventual building users to determine when the building developer is ready to buy.
  • For example if the land development takes 2 years and the building development takes 2 years, the eventual users are 4 years away from occupying the space.
  • What is the user demand four years from now?

Will the current market conditions continue or improve over the land development and sell-out period?

  • Once the land developer begins construction of the horizontal improvements, it is virtually impossible to stop construction without serious financial losses.
  • The favorable market conditions must last long enough for the land developer to finish construction and sell the finished pads to the building developers.
  • This is probably at 2-4 year window.

How will competitive properties respond to the new development?

  • Competitive properties can respond in may ways:
    • Lower rents or increase inducemen
    • Increase marketing efforts.
    • Start new construction project.
    • Delay planned construction.

Who are the active building developers in the market and how can they be approached?

  • Land developers sell their lots to a finite universe of possible building developers.
  • Building developers are in competition with each other.
  • Personal relationships from earlier projects are the best way to approach building developers with a new opportunity.
  • Building developers must be assured that they will be treated fairly, no last look.
