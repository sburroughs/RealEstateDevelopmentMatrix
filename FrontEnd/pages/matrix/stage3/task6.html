Improvements â€“ Land Development (VI.3.)

Focus on the engineering of the approved infrastructure and common areas to obtain the necessary approvals.

  • Detailed plans and specification that can be approved by authorities.
  • Bid-out proposed improvements.
  • Detailed plans and specifications from which costs and bids can be estimated.
  • Post the required performance and fiduciary bonds.
  • Performance bonds: guarantee that the improvements will be completed.
  • Fiduciary bonds: guarantee that constructed improvements will hold up.

Contract to have the infrastructure and common area constructed on-time and on budget.

  • Multiple contractors for:
  • Wet utilities: water, sewer, gas
  • Dry utilities: electric telephone, communications
  • Road construction
  • Traffic control systems
  • Special projects
  • Off-site improvements
  • Trunk sewer lines
  • Sewer lift stations
  • Road intersection and traffic calming improvements
  • Transit stations
  • Parks and recreational areas

Stage, manage and control the land development construction.

  • Scheduling is all-important; re-scheduling is to be expected.
  • Reserves for contingencies and delays.
  • Citizens safety and convenience.
  • Actual vs Budget reports.
